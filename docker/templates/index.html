Why these prompts?

These examples are designed to exercise Agentic Behavior. They combine:

  • Persona: Requiring the model to stay in character (Mad Hatter).
  • Reasoning: Requiring the model to identify a problem that needs solving.
  • Tool Use: Forcing the model to use the calculate() tool for math, rather than guessing.
Watch how the trained model maintains its "mad" persona while correctly executing the math logic!
