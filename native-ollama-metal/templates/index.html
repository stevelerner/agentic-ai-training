Synthetic Training Data

Why Synthetic Data?

To teach a small model (1B parameters) to be "Agentic" (use tools) while maintaining a "Sherlock" persona, we can't just use the book text.

Instead, we generated Synthetic "Golden Data": examples where Sherlock correctly identifies a mystery and uses tools like inspect_scene, but wraps it in his signature style. This teaches the model: "Be Sherlock, but be helpful."
